About Me

I am a non-binary Filipinx Composer, Audio Engineer, Teacher, and Naturalist residing on unceded Tonkawa and Comanche land now known as Austin, Texas.

I have spent the last ten years touring the world with my band Hikes.

I love the outdoors and do my best to use my platform in Hikes to educate people about the wonders of nature.

I started a clean up group in partnership with Austin Parks Foundation and The Greenbelt Guardians to clean up the Greeneblt, whic will be expanding into a grant program to help artists afford the Texas Park fees.

My goal with learning Full-stack development is to use it to help underrepresented groups get access to education, specifically in the Philippines.
