ABOUT ME

I am a Filipinx Composer, Audio Engineer, Teacher, and Naturalist residing on unceded Tonkawa and Comanche land commonly known as Austin, Texas.


I have spent the last ten years touring the world with my band Hikes.


I love the outdoors and use my platform in Hikes to educate people about the wonders of nature. This is the fundamental value at the core of Hikes.


I started a clean-up group in partnership with Austin Parks Foundation and The Greenbelt Guardians to clean up the Greenebelt, which will be expanding into a grant program to help artists afford the Texas Park fees.


My goal with learning Full-stack development is to use it to help underrepresented communities get access to nature and education. My dream is to one day help children with limited resources in rural Philippines.
